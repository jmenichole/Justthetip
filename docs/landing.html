Why Trustless Matters

  • One Signature, All Tokens: A single wallet signature proves ownership of your Solana address. Since all tokens (SOL, USDC, BONK, USDT) share the same address, you only sign once to enable tipping with any token. No per-token registrations needed!
  • No Repeated Signatures: Unlike traditional bots that require signing every transaction, our trustless agent verifies your wallet once and enables frictionless tipping across all supported tokens.
  • Currently Supporting SOL: SOL tipping is fully implemented and working now. Multi-token support (USDC, BONK, USDT) is in active development as part of our x402 Hackathon submission‚Äîsame registration, more tokens coming!
  • Smart Contract Escrow: Funds for unregistered users are held in program-derived addresses until they claim, ensuring no tips are lost.
  • Zero Trust Required: All operations are cryptographically verifiable on-chain. No need to trust the bot operator‚Äîverify everything yourself!
  • x402 Integration: Premium features available via USDC micropayments, enabling advanced analytics, priority processing, and enhanced functionality.
